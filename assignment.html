Sunway Lagoon Waterpark

About

Sunway Lagoon
one of Pakistan's premier Water Park. It is located just 5 minutes from Gharo Town and 50 Minutes from Karachi International Airport (subject to traffic) on 42 beautifully landscaped acres, with huge lush green coconut trees,garden,comfortable and covered food court with dining tables & chairs. More than 25 Exciting water Slides & 12 Swimming Pools. The Quality of water in our Park is the best ,continuously treated and filtered through the most advance filtration System imported from Spain, keeping water sparkling blue, Sunway Lagoon can be compared with any of the international standard Park all over the world.


Our Attractions


OCEAN LAGOON

Pakistan’s Largest Wave Pool, equipped with the World’s latest wave machine, capable of making 24 different types of the craziest waves! Now enjoy 10 different waves an hour!

KIDS MANIA

Great fun for kids of all ages, Kidz Mania has all the adventures kids love! They can play on any one (or all) of the 6 slides, get drenched under the giant water bucket, or have a water fight with their friends! Kidz Mania is sure to please the kids and those who are kids at heart!

WET N' WILD DANCE FLOOR

Come dance to the latest and greatest music with the water! The Wet N’ Wild Dance Floor is sure to get you groovin’ and movin’! Only available at Sunway Lagoon Pakistan!
